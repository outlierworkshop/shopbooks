Import Amazon orders (CSV)

Get it from Amazon → Account → Request My Data → "Your Orders" (emailed as Retail.OrderHistory.*.csv). Builds an itemized receipt per order and auto-matches to your card charges by amount + date. Heads-up: Amazon bills per shipment, so an order total won't always equal one charge - unmatched ones wait on the Receipts list for you to match by hand.
