Claude (cloud)

Get a key at console.anthropic.com - costs are typically pennies per statement. Most accurate, especially for messy receipts and statements. Data is sent to Anthropic.

Ollama (local)

Runs entirely on this machine - nothing leaves it. Install Ollama from ollama.com, then pull a vision model, e.g. ollama pull llama3.2-vision (or qwen2.5vl, often better at receipts). Needs a recent Ollama (structured outputs) and a GPU for speed. Save your settings first, then test:
